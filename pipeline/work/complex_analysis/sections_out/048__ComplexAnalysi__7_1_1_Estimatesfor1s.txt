1.1 Estimates for 1/ζ(s)

of the zeta function near the line Re(s) = 1; the basic object involved is
the logarithmic derivative ζ′(s)/ζ(s). For this reason, besides the non-
vanishing of ζ on the line, we need to know about the growth of ζ′
and 1/ζ. The former was dealt with in Proposition 2.7 of Chapter 6; we
now treat the latter.
