1. Show that the free product G ∗H of nontrivial groups G and H has trivial center,
and that the only elements of G ∗H of ﬁnite order are the conjugates of ﬁnite-order
elements of G and H .
2. Let X ⊂Rm be the union of convex open sets X1, ··· , Xn such that Xi∩Xj ∩Xk ≠∅
for all i, j, k. Show that X is simply-connected.
3. Show that the complement of a ﬁnite set of points in Rn is simply-connected if
n ≥3.
