roundabout route:

(2) Specialize this to the case of bundles with п¬Ғber a disk, yielding a basic result

(3) Show this applies to all orientable disk bundles.
(4) Deduce the Gysin sequence by plugging the Thom isomorphism into the long
exact sequence of cohomology groups for the pair consisting of a disk bundle
and its boundary sphere bundle.
