Steenrod Powers
Our remaining task is to verify the axioms and Adem relations for the Steenrod
powers for an odd prime p. Unfortunately this is quite a bit more complicated than
the p = 2 case, largely because one has to be very careful in computing the many
coefﬁcients in Zp that arise. Even for the innocent-looking axiom P 0 = 11 it will take
three pages to calculate the normalization constants needed to make the axiom hold.
One could wish that the whole process was a lot cleaner.
