The Homotopy Extension Property
In this п¬Ғnal section of the chapter we will actually prove a few things. In partic-
ular we prove the two criteria for homotopy equivalence described above, along with
the fact that any two homotopy equivalent spaces can be embedded as deformation
retracts of the same space.

extension property. Thus (X, A) has the homotopy extension property if every map
